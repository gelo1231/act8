Mt. Zion Pilgrim Mountain is a popular tourist destination in the town of Bugallon, Pangasinan. It is a sprawling complex of religious and natural attractions, including a statue of Jesus Christ that resembles the Christ the Redeemer statue in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Mt. Zion Pilgrim Mountain is located in Barangay Laguit, Bugallon, Pangasinan. It is a 52 hectares lot, developed as a pilgrimage site by the Emmanuel Servants of the Holy Trinity (ESHT) Congregation. The place is very peaceful, and it is perfect for meditation. Mt. Zion Pilgrim Mountain is a place of peace and tranquility. It is a place where visitors can come to reflect on their faith and connect with nature. If you are looking for a place to relax and rejuvenate, Mt. Zion Pilgrim Mountain is the perfect destination.
